#+title: Role (IAM)

A role is a collection of permissions that allow you to interact in some
way with a cloud resource.

A [[id:452805d5-6c0b-4cb1-a8b5-cb1955adc50c][CSP]] will often have a bunch of canned roles for you to use, but you can
generally make your own.
